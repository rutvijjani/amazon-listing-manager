Guide
Recommended process for more accounts
  1. Keep your current active account connected.
  2. Add the next seller account with marketplace + seller ID.
  3. Repeat for more accounts, even in the same marketplace.
  4. Switch active account before searching or editing listings.
Important notes
  • Listings/search/update always run against the active seller account.
  • Same marketplace can now have multiple seller IDs.
  • Different regions can require different seller IDs or authorizations.
  • If Amazon returns product-type errors, load the item again after switching account.
